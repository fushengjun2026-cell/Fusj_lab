The long version

Curiosity is a practice, not a personality trait.

I am currently studying at Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, where I am building a foundation in computer science while following the fast-moving edge of artificial intelligence.

What interests me most is the moment a technical possibility becomes a human experience. I like translating complex systems into clear interfaces, testing ideas through small prototypes, and keeping a written trail of what worked, what failed, and what changed my mind.

Outside the classroom, I spend time with generative AI, machine learning, web development, visual design, and the wider questions around how technology shapes attention and agency. I am still early in the journey, which is exactly why I want to keep exploring in public.
